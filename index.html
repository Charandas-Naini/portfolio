About

I'm a developer with a passion for building fun, high-quality game experiences.

Currently, I'm a Senior Game Developer at YsecIT Softwares and working on 3D virtual sports betting games, including horse racing, sprinting, cricket, and other simulations.

Previously, I specialized in 2D mobile games, creating popular match-3, connect, tap, and puzzle-based titles. My work has spanned from casual and arcade to stylized puzzle games, and I’ve developed gameplay systems, UI, and tools to support reusable and efficient workflows across projects.

I primarily work in Unity (C#) and have experience with custom tools, animation systems, particle effects, level design, and optimization for mobile and standalone platforms. I enjoy solving complex gameplay challenges and care deeply about user experience and technical quality.

When I’m not developing, I’m usually exploring new games, prototyping concepts, or diving into game feel and design theory to keep growing as a developer.
